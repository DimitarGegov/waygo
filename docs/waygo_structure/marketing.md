## Why This Demographic Focus?

### Target Demographic Rationale

| Demographic | Why Target | Value |
|-------------|------------|-------|
| **Adults with kids under 8** | Year-round locals, active family activities, café/museum culture, educational value for kids | High LTV, no seasonality |
| **15-18 year olds** | Free time (afterschool, weekends), social media native, group-oriented, exploration curiosity, school holiday bursts | Organic growth, word-of-mouth |

### Benefits of This Pivot

1. **Year-round engagement** - Locals don't have tourism seasonal dips
2. **Differentiation** - Builds local community loyalty vs tourist-dependent apps
3. **Family-friendly quests** - Mixed-age group activities
4. **Predictable activity spikes** - School holidays (Nov, Feb, Apr, Jun)
5. **Word-of-mouth** - Parents recommend to parents, students recommend to students
