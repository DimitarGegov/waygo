# Waygo - Explore Plovdiv

## What is Waygo?

Waygo is a mobile app that turns exploring Plovdiv, Bulgaria into an adventure game. Think of it like a treasure hunt mixed with a city guide - you discover cool places, complete quests, earn rewards, and compete with other explorers.

## The Core Idea

Imagine you love coffee and want to find the best cafes in Plovdiv. Waygo shows you a map where you can:
- Discover hidden gems around the city
- Complete fun challenges that take you to multiple places
- Earn points, badges, and discounts along the way
- See how you compare to other explorers on a leaderboard

## How It Works

### 1. Exploring the Map
When you open Waygo, you see a dark-themed map of Plovdiv. Some areas are "foggy" - they're hidden until you visit them. As you explore, the fog clears and reveals what's around you.

### 2. Visiting Places
Every place on the map is a partner - a business that opted in. This includes:
- Cafes (like Kapana Craft Beer Bar, Coffee Trail Cafe)
- Museums (Regional History Museum, Art Gallery, Ethnographic Museum)
- Cultural sites (Dzhumaya Mosque, Roman Stadium Ruins)
- Restaurants (Old Town Bistro)

### 3. Checking In
When you visit a place, you "check in" by:
- Being physically near the location (the app checks your GPS)
- Entering a short code provided by the business

### 4. Completing Quests
Quests are themed challenges that guide you through the city:
- "The Coffee Trail" - Visit 3 coffee spots, earn 150 points
- "History Hunter" - Explore 5 museums and cultural sites, earn 300 points
- "Plovdiv Grand Tour" - Visit all 8 major attractions, earn 500 points

### 5. Earning Rewards
As you explore, you earn:
- **XP (Experience Points)** - The more you explore, the more you earn
- **Levels** - Every 500 XP = level up
- **Streaks** - Visit at least one place daily to keep your streak going
- **Badges** - Special achievements like "Coffee Lover" or "Week Warrior"
- **Vouchers** - Discount codes from participating businesses

### 6. Competing
The leaderboard shows top explorers - weekly, monthly, and all-time rankings.

## Who's It For?

### For Explorers (Regular Users)
People who want to discover Plovdiv in a fun, gamified way.

### For Businesses (Partners)
Local cafes, museums, restaurants get visibility and customers.

### For Admins
Manage users, businesses, and quests.

## Why This Exists

Plovdiv is a beautiful city with rich history, but many visitors only see the main tourist spots. Waygo encourages people to:
- Venture into neighborhoods like Kapana
- Discover local businesses
- Experience the city's culture authentically
- Get rewarded for exploration

It's like Pokemon GO but for city exploration.

## Summary

Waygo = Maps + Gamification + Local Business

A fun way to explore a city, get rewarded for it, and support local businesses.
