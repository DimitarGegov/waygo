# Waygo - Executive Presentation

---

## The Pitch

**Waygo** is a gamified city exploration app that turns discovering Plovdiv into an adventure game.

*Explore like a game. Earn real rewards. Discover authentic Plovdiv.*

---

## The Problem

- **70%** of tourists visit only top 5 attractions
- Local businesses invisible against Google Maps chains
- No engaging way to find authentic local experiences
- Tourism economy flows to chains, not locals

---

## The Solution

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Fog-of-War Map** | Hidden areas revealed on exploration |
| **Quests** | Themed challenges (Coffee Trail, History Hunter) |
| **Gamification** | XP, levels, streaks, badges |
| **Vouchers** | Real discounts from partners |
| **Leaderboards** | Social competition |
